Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to fight through endless waves of monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character!

Game Details

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced action survival game where you'll face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the more challenging it becomes. Collect experience gems to level up and unlock powerful new abilities and upgrades!

This game combines elements of roguelike progression with arcade-style gameplay. Each run is unique as you choose different abilities and face different combinations of enemies.

Monster Survivors features beautiful pixel art graphics, fluid animations, and an exciting soundtrack that keeps you engaged throughout your survival journey.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically in the direction of nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems (green orbs) to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up
  • Avoid getting hit by enemies to survive longer
  • Defeat special enemies for bonus items and power-ups

The game gets progressively harder over time, with stronger enemies and boss encounters. How long can you survive?
