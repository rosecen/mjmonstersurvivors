Survivors Classic is the original survival game that started it all. Navigate through dangerous environments, upgrade your character, and outlast the competition!

Game Details

Survivors Classic is the game that defined the genre. In this classic survival experience, you'll face waves of enemies in a minimalist but challenging environment.

The game features a unique progression system where your character evolves over time based on the upgrades you choose. With each run being different, you'll discover new strategies and combinations to maximize your survival time.

Survivors Classic has a retro-inspired aesthetic with clean, distinctive visuals that make it easy to track enemies and projectiles even during the most chaotic moments.

How to Play

  • Move with WASD or arrow keys
  • Auto-attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies
  • Choose new weapons or upgrades when leveling up
  • Avoid enemy contact to maintain health
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves

The game features multiple character classes, each with unique starting weapons and abilities. Can you discover the most powerful combinations?
