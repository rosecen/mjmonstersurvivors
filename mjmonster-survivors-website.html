About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelite survival game where you:

  • Battle endless waves of unique monsters
  • Collect powerful magical relics and upgrades
  • Unlock 10+ unique characters with special abilities
  • Survive procedurally generated nightmare realms

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. The game automatically attacks nearby enemies. Collect XP gems to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult monster waves!
