Poki Survivors is a fast-paced survival adventure with unique gameplay mechanics. Test your skills and reflexes in this action-packed game!

Game Details

Poki Survivors offers a modern take on the survival genre with enhanced graphics and innovative gameplay mechanics. Survive against waves of unique enemies while collecting power-ups and special abilities.

The game features a dynamic difficulty system that adapts to your skill level, ensuring an engaging experience for both beginners and veterans alike. With multiple playable characters, each with their own strengths and weaknesses, every playthrough offers a fresh challenge.

Vibrant environments and detailed enemy designs make Poki Survivors visually stunning, while the immersive sound effects and music create an engaging atmosphere.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Attacks trigger automatically against nearby enemies
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your weapons and abilities
  • Choose wisely between different upgrades when leveling up
  • Special abilities can be activated with the spacebar
  • Watch for elite enemies that drop rare enhancements

The game features an achievement system that rewards different playstyles and strategies. Can you unlock all the secret characters and abilities?
